The Enterprise

Agricultural Mastery in Iraklio, Crete

Located in the fertile heart of Iraklio Kritis, Petraki Single Member P.C. is a specialized leader in the Farm Product Raw Material Merchant Wholesalers sector. Under the strategic direction of Key Principal Sofia Petraki, we bridge the gap between regional agricultural excellence and international markets.

Operating from our headquarters at 5 Ainikolioti, we specialize in the high-volume distribution of grain and field beans. We believe that agricultural trade is a technical science, requiring absolute logistical precision and a deep respect for raw material integrity.

As a robust representative of the Cretan Wholesale Trade sector with $10.76 million in annual revenue, we leverage our strategic location to deliver high-performance supply chain solutions.
